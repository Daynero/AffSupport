# Local Video Compressor — test guide

## How to install
Download the DMG, open it, and drag **Local Video Compressor Agent** to Applications.

## If macOS shows a warning
Control-click the app, choose **Open**, then confirm **Open**. Never disable macOS security.

## Connect and compress
Open the app. It opens the website and connects automatically. Choose **Select videos**, then **Start compression**. Results are saved beside each original unless you choose another folder. Videos never leave your Mac.

## Quit and report a problem
Quit the app from the Dock or Activity Monitor. On the website choose **Copy diagnostics** and send that text with a short description. The report excludes videos and full private paths.
